Fun for the whole family

Enjoy your family outing with these fun and crafty side activities! Explore your way through the jungles more than 65 million years ago where dangerous dinosaurs came to life, ride on our baby herbivores such as the triceratops and Diplodocus around the wilderness or have a go at the Origami Shop where you have a hands-on experience creating your favourite dinosaurs! More fun-packed activities at the park itself for you to discover!

  • Jurassic Fruit Salad
  • Dinosaur Chop

Tempt your taste buds with amazing, savory dishes and snacks at the park! No matter where you go, you are sure to find at least one store to fill your stomachs. Try out our delicious Jurassic Fruit Salad made with fresh and nutritious fruits and the tender Dinosaur Chop you can never say no to. Rest assured it is not actually dinosaur meat but pork meat so delicious its extinction is nearing.
