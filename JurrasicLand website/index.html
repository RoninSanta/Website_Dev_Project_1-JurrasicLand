rides and attractions

Step into Jurassic Era and stand in awe and wonder at a land transformed by science after 65 million years. But don't be deceived by the island’s beauty because danger lurks behind every corner. For those brave enough to visit the raptor paddock, you may be able to carefully approach one of these deadly predators. Then feel the rush of the hunt as you race through the jungle alongside raptors on the JurassicLand VelociCoaster, a new species of roller coaster coming Summer 2021.
